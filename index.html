Problem Size iOS App

Problem Size helps young children, including kids with ADHD, autism, and anxiety, learn to tell the difference between a small hiccup and a real emergency! These Social Stories and feedback can help kids strengthen their social skills, cost-free.

Download Now!

Watch Social Stories

Kids can watch an animated story about an everyday moment, like forgetting a water bottle or messing up before a performance. After watching the clip, our AI buddy Bolt will prompt them to select the appropriate size of the problem!

Glitch, Bummer, Disaster

Glitch

No one is in danger, and it can be fixed fast, or there is an easy backup plan.

Bummer

No one is in danger, but it can't be fixed fast, and it needs time or help.

Disaster

Someone could get badly hurt, or it could cost a lot of money to fix.

Feedback from Bolt

After each answer, our AI-assisted feedback bot, Bolt, will explain whether a child's answer was correct based on the context and outcome of the story!

Practice!

Kids can practice in Daily mode with 5 social stories, or Challenge mode to take on 15 social stories!

Track Progress

Problem Size tracks kids' current XP and awards as they explore more stories, while keeping a streak with a practice calendar! The Parent Dashboard also allows guardians to see an overview of their child's progress, identifying strong areas and room for improvement!
